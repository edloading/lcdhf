Services

Here are the main services we provide:

Free Diabetes Screening & Monitoring

Free Weight Checks & Monitoring, daily

Free Blood Pressure Screening & Monitoring

To achieve our objectives of providing free basic health services to patients in needy communities, we use various approaches. At our free Health Centers in Africa, we do free diabetes, high blood pressure, and weight checks. We provide unlimited checks daily. These services are carried out by paid medical professionals and volunteer physicians who assist patients with abnormal readings.

We carry out mini health fairs at village health centers and other areas in the community where our basic health services are needed. During our health fairs, we provide medications and other medical supplies.

We provide diabetic monitoring supplies to needy individuals who cannot make it to our center or mini fairs. These supplies include test strips, lancets, test kits, blood pressure machines, and alcohol pads.

Our services are sometimes provided to the patients we serve through our partners. We have partnered with other non-profit organizations and institutions to carry out our mission of SAVING LIVES ONE CHECK AT A TIME.
